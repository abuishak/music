Home Page.

It is a family-owned business that has been in existence since 1955. Sam O'Mally Jr. is the second generation to manage and operate this music store. They specialize in traditional music store needs like band equipment, keyboards, sheet music and musical instrument repair and instruction. We sell new and used instruments. Private lessons are offered by area musicians. You may call for more information on lessons or to set up an appointment. They have always strived to give personal assistance to their customers and to go the extra mile to make sure every customer is a satisfied one.
